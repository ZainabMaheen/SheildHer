Learn More About ShieldHer

A smart system designed to protect women’s privacy and ensure safety in moments that matter most.

Hidden Camera Detection

ShieldHer uses a dual-layer detection system. Wi-Fi scanning identifies suspicious online cameras, while infrared (IR) reflection detects hidden or offline cameras by locating lens reflections invisible to the human eye.

When a camera is detected, the keychain instantly alerts the user using a red LED and beep sound.

“Not Feeling Safe” SOS Feature

In emergency situations, opening a phone may not be practical. ShieldHer provides a one-tap SOS button on the keychain that sends live location alerts to trusted contacts instantly.

This ensures faster response and immediate support when it is needed most.

Community Safety Map

All detected unsafe locations appear on a live community safety map. Users can view reports of hidden cameras, SOS alerts, and unsafe areas before entering a location.

This turns individual detection into collective protection.

Web Dashboard & App

The ShieldHer app and web dashboard allow users to log in securely, view detection history, manage trusted contacts, access safety alerts, and stay informed about nearby risks.

Everything is designed to be simple, fast, and reliable.

ShieldHer helps reduce the risk of blackmail, harassment, and hidden surveillance — giving women confidence and control over their safety.
