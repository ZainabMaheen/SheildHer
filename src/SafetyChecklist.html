Pre-Travel Safety Checklist

  • Review hotel/accommodation reviews for security issues
  • Save emergency contact numbers locally
  • Enable location sharing with trusted contacts

Room Inspection Checklist

Check rooms upon arrival at hotels or rentals

0%

0 of 7

  • Scan entire room with flashlight for lens reflections
  • Check mirrors for two-way mirror indicators
  • Inspect light fixtures, smoke detectors, and vents
  • Check bathroom for suspicious devices or holes
  • Examine decorative items and electronics
  • Look for WiFi networks with suspicious names
  • Check door locks and security features
